What is Medja

One app to run the whole business

Nigerian cleaning companies juggle phone calls, WhatsApp groups and paper job cards to run jobs, and lose money to untracked cash, missed invoices and no-shows. Medja replaces all of it with a single, mobile-first system the owner and every cleaner can use, and it collects money the Nigerian way.

The problem it solves

Where cleaning businesses leak money

🗂️

Paper job cards get lost

No record of what was cleaned, by whom, or what the client agreed to pay. Disputes end in write-offs.

💬

WhatsApp isn't a schedule

Owners burn hours chasing teams by call; nobody knows who is at which site.

💸

Cash goes untracked

Field cash, unmatched transfers, forgotten invoices, and global tools can't even collect naira.

The insight

Global apps like Jobber, ZenMaid and Housecall Pro have zero Nigerian payment support: no Paystack, no bank transfer, no USSD. Medja is built naira-first, WhatsApp-native and Android-first for exactly this market.
