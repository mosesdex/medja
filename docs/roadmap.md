# Medja Roadmap

Four phases; the founder's company starts using the app at the end of Phase 1 and pilots each phase in real operations before the next begins. Full detail in the [design spec](superpowers/specs/2026-07-11-medja-saas-design.md).

## Phase 1 — Operate *(owner can run the business)*
Auth & company onboarding → client CRM (sites, access notes) → job calendar → digital job cards with per-type checklists (residential / commercial / post-construction) → quote builder (room-count naira templates) → invoices → WhatsApp share → owner dashboard.

**Exit test:** founder schedules and invoices a real week of jobs in Medja.

## Phase 2 — Field *(cleaners on the app)*
Cleaner "My Jobs" view → dispatch with conflict warnings → status pipeline → GPS check-in/out → before/after photos → on-site checklists → staff vetting profiles (NIN/ID + guarantor) → verified-cleaner share.

**Exit test:** a real job completed end-to-end by a cleaner on their own phone, with photos the client receives.

## Phase 3 — Money
Paystack payment links → cash logging → deposits → payment reminders → recurring contracts + auto job generation → auto monthly contract invoicing → virtual-account transfer reconciliation → expenses.

**Exit test:** a client pays a Medja invoice via Paystack; a monthly contract invoices itself.

## Phase 4 — Scale
Attendance from GPS → payroll runs → public booking link → client ratings → reports → multi-site rollups → WhatsApp Business API notifications → SaaS billing & plan limits → offline hardening.

**Exit test:** a second cleaning company onboards self-serve and subscribes on a paid plan.
