The product

Management first. Selling second.

Exactly the priority in the brief — and the priority the research supports. She's not asking for a website; she's asking to stop losing money she can't see.

🧾

Know your money

One-tap sale logging (cash or transfer), daily takings, expenses, and rough profit in plain language — no accounting required.

📦

Know your stock

Variant inventory (size × colour) plus a one-of-one thrift mode. Auto-decrements on every sale; alerts before you run out.

📒

Know who owes you

A live debtor ledger with one-tap WhatsApp reminders. Credit selling is a Nigerian reality — Boutika makes the debt collectable.

🔒

Know your staff

Each attendant logs under their own PIN; every sale, discount and stock edit is attributed — the verified defence against shrinkage.

🛍️

Sell everywhere

Your catalog becomes hername.boutika.shop with WhatsApp checkout — a toggle, not a second app. Orders drop back into stock automatically.

🧳

Restock smarter

A trip planner ranks re-orders by what actually sold and margin — turning the owner's biggest cash gamble into a shopping list.
